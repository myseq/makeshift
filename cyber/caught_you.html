⚠️ Cybersecurity Awareness Exercise

You scanned a demo QR code!

Don't worry—this is a safe simulation conducted for Cybersecurity Awareness Month. No malicious code was run, and none of your personal data was stored.

What an attacker could have captured:

  • Device Details: Operating system, browser version, and screen resolution.
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on your public IP address.
  • Credentials: Any usernames or passwords entered if this were a fake login page.

How to stay safe in the future:

1
Preview the link: Always read the full URL popup on your phone's camera before opening it.
2
Check for physical overlays: Feel QR codes on public posters/meters to ensure it isn't a sticker placed over the real code.
3
Use native camera tools: Stick to built-in iOS/Android camera apps rather than downloading unverified third-party scanner applications.
